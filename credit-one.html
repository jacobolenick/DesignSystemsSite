Credit One Bank

I worked on building a 0-1 design system for C1B's internal tools and it saved the company hundreds of thousands of dollars and increased work efficiency by 40%.

The Challenge

Credit One Bank needed a comprehensive design system to unify their internal tools and streamline the development process across multiple teams.

The Solution

We developed a modular design system that included:

  • Comprehensive component library
  • Design tokens for consistent styling
  • Documentation for designers and developers
  • Automated workflow processes

Impact

The implementation of the design system resulted in:

  • 40% increase in development efficiency
  • Significant cost savings in development time
  • Improved consistency across all internal tools
  • Faster onboarding for new team members
